Mathematical Core

The most advanced DBS engine available

BubbleScheduler's engine is built on the Bienstock-Zuckerberg LP relaxation — the gold standard for large-scale open pit scheduling. The mixed-integer programming formulation is solved either by our native LP engine or delegated to enterprise-grade solvers like Gurobi and CPLEX.

Unlike traditional scheduling approaches that rely on pre-computed ultimate pit limits, BubbleScheduler operates directly on the block model — maximizing NPV without the loss of resolution inherent in nested shell methods.
